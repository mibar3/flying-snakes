How can we help you?

Click on a question to see the answer.


How can I log in?

You can log in by clicking on the 'sign up'-button at the navbar.
To log in you have to provide your email address and your password. If you don't have a password yet,, please sign up first. You can find the button for signing up also at the Navbar.

What do I need to sign up

To sign up at our reservation system, we need your Email, a password and your Address.
Now you can login everytime with your email and your password to choose your seat.

How does the reservation of my seat works?

To choose and reserve your favorite seat on the plane you have to sign up or login into your account first.
Afterwards you will be automatically transfered to the reservation tool. Here you can choose your seat. Seats marked with a X are already taken. Don't forget to confirm your choice
