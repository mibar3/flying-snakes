How can we help you?

Click on the question to see the answer.


How can I log in?

You can log in by clicking on the 'login'-button at the top or click here.
To log in you have to provide your email address and your password. If you don't have a password yet, please sign up first. You can find the button for signing up also at the top or click here

What do I need to sign up?

To sign up at our reservation system, we just need you name and you email-Adress. Besides this we need you to create your username and a password
After you are registered you only need to login with your email and your password to choose your seat. You can find the form to register at the top under 'sign up' or click here

How does the reservation of my seat works?

To choose and reserve your favorite seat on the plane you have to sign up or log in first.
Afterwards you will be automatically transferred to the reservation tool. Here you can choose your seat. Your selected Seat is marked in a different color. Seats with a X are already taken. Don't forget to confirm your choice at the end with the confirm button.

What happens after I confirmed my seats?

As soon as you confirmed your Seat choice, you will be transferred to the confirmed page. Like this you know, that your reservation worked.
Please check now your Email, to get a copy of the reservation.

I don't like my seats. How can I change them?

If you are unhappy with your seat choice, just send us an Email and tell us why you want another seat. We can cancel your reservation for you.
After we canceled your reservation, you need to make a new reservation. For that login and choose your new seat(s). Don't forget to confirm at the end.

How can I cancel my seats?

To cancel your seats, please send us an Email. We are going to cancel your reservation for you.
